title: BYOK is a feature, not a compromise
description: Bring-your-own-key is one of the cleanest ways to make Logos AI sustainable and credible.
pubDate: 2026-03-26
---

Bring-your-own-key is not a backup plan. It is a product feature.

It gives technical users what they actually want:

- control over provider choice
- visibility into usage
- flexibility around models and limits

It also gives Logos AI something equally important: a sane cost structure.

The positioning should stay simple:

> Use your own API key for maximum control and flexibility.

That language feels strong because it is about agency, not apology.
